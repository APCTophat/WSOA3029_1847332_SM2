"In the darkest times, hope is something you give yourself. That is the meaning of inner strength - Uncle Iroh, Avatar the last Airbender"

For my final graph, I decided that I wanted to focus on hope, so I used a quote from one of my favourite fictional characters of all time. I believe that through data visualisations people search for hope. I wanted to play around with this idea with this graph, however, I wanted to focus on the people who have Covid-19 being the ones having to give themselves hope. For it up to those who have the disease to give themselves hope.

To do this I made a graph that shows three pieces of information about Covid-19 in South Africa. The X-axis is the Date, like all my other graphs, I am using the change over several months to illustrate trends in data. The Y-axis is the Fatality rate of Covid-19 during those times, to work this out I dived the current deaths to the current confirmed cases of each date. The higher up the graph the circle the more likely a person would die from Covid-19. Finally, I wanted to indicate the number of people who actively had the disease at that time. To indicate this, the size of the circles correlates to the number of people that had Covid-19 on that day.

Finally, I added an index that will display this information when the circles are hovered over. Giving the exact number of each point of data I am representing. Finally, I tried to make sure the graph looks the same throughout different sizes and all data is visible
